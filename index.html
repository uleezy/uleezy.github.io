Meet The Ulanator...

I'm just me, I be making sounds and stuff. I make all kinds of sounds but I'm deeply rooted in G-funk and glitch-hop and then whatever else I'm listening to at that time. I started making music back in 2017 and now I finally feel confident enough to properly make my debut.

Anything and everything feeds into what I do. I'm half nerd, half ghetto fabulous — a So-Cal Samoan out of San Diego. I produce, compose and sing a bit, but don't get it twisted... I ain't no poet, I'm a m**********g rapper.
